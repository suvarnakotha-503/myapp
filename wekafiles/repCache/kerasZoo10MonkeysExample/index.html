kerasZoo10MonkeysExample: Provides a Knowledge Flow template example for training a DL model on the Kaggle 10 monkeys data

Author:Mark Hall
Maintainer:Weka team <wekalist{[at]}list.scms.waikato.ac.nz>

Provides a Knowledge Flow template example for training a DL model on the Kaggle 10 monkeys image data. Includes the data along with training and validation metadata arff files. Once installed, the example can be found in the Knowledge Flow's "template" menu (i.e. third button from the right in the toolbar).
