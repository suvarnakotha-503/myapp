Category:Attribute selection
Date:2021-12-16
Depends:weka (>=3.8.3)
Description:Evaluates the worth of an attribute i by computing the mean of the worths (using CfsSubsetEval) of the attribute subsets composed of attribute i and each of the other attributes. Attributes with low correlation to other attributes and highly correlated with the class are preferred. For more information see: F. Jimenez, G. Sanchez, J. Palma, L. Miralles-Pechuan, J. Botia (2021). Multivariate feature ranking of gene expression data, ArXiv, 2111.02357
